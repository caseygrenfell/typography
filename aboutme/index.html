Allow me to introduce myself...

Casey Grenfell

Origin Story

In the winter of '91 I made my debut on planet earth on what I'm told was a rather mild Thursday in South Africa. I grew up in beautiful (boiling) Durban until emigrating to the UK with the rest of my family in 2006. After spending most of my time here living in Berkshire, I finally discovered and fell in love with Oxfordshire, and my partner and I are now happily settled in Abingdon.


My Hobbies


One of my favourite and longest hobbies is art and crafts, pictured are a few of my recent paintings (many unfinished as has become the habit), but I also like crochet and sculpture.

When I was 23 I started a small business called Pastel Pets where I painted pet portraits for clients and crocheted custom teddy bears which I sold on commission in a few independent shops in England and Wales. Nowadays, I tend only to pick up a paintbrush for therapeautic reasons. Watercolours are my favourite medium.



One evening in November '18, I packed this small backpack and booked a flight to Amsterdam on a whim.

I spent three days going museum hopping, sketching the canal views outside rustic cafes, and trawling the street markets.

It was beautiful, but absolutely
freezing!

My Life


I have three children; Chase, Ryder and Jessica, who keep me constantly on my toes! They each have unique personalities and are a joy to look after (as a grown woman, it's also great to have an excuse to go see Pixar films at the Cinema).


I love cooking, especially Italian. My grandparents were of that origin and I'm certain the taste in food is genetic! My favourite dish to cook is mushroom risotto (here's one I made earlier), it takes a little bit of work during cooking, but I promise it's worth it. Find the recipe here.

My family and I are very close, I have two younger (and taller) sisters, and my mother who lives in Berkshire. She runs several pubs in the area and we regularly meet for gins and a natter.
